QAP 6.1.2: Procedure for Control of Non-Compliances


Q.Management Review committee in consultation with CEO/Director to analyze the non-compliance and decide on the action plan to be prepared. Yes No
Q.Head of departments should identify preventive actions to eliminate potential non-conformities. Record of non-conformities will be periodically monitored and reviewed. Yes No

> Attendance:
Q.Trainees shall obtain 90% attendance in all subjects to attend for final assessment. Yes No
Q.Issue notice to trainees who fails to obtain 90% attendance and he/she shall not be allowed to seat for assessment Yes No
Q.If the trainees fails to obtain 90% attendance shall be allowed to seat for assessment except where absence is authorized in exceptional circumstances or with the strong evidences of the failures. Yes No
Q.Refer terms and condition of the attendances. Yes No
