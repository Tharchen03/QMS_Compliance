QAP 7.1.1: Procedure for conducting Internal Audit


Q.The Internal Auditor should prepare the Audit Plan considering the importance of each department and the previous audit findings. Yes No
Q.The Audit Plan should indicate the month and the corresponding department for auditing. Yes No
Q.Prior to each audit the Internal Auditor should inform the concerned staff on the Audit Plan and scope of the Audit Yes No
Q.The Internal Auditor should study the following Documents: Quality Manual, Relevant Procedures, Forms & Records, Previous Audit report/findings, List of corrective actions to be taken. Yes No
Q.The Internal Auditor should submit the report to the Principal to review and prepare corrective and preventive Action Plan. Yes No
Q.The Internal Auditor should schedule follow up Audits based on the importance of the activity and the status of Audit findings. Yes No
Q.Quality Audits are carried out half yearly to cover all the departments in the Institute. Yes No
Q.Corrective and preventive actions are discussed at the Management Review meeting Yes No
Q.Concerned staff should write the proposed corrective actions and the date of completion of such actions. Yes No
Q.Principal should the take necessary follow up actions. Yes No
Q.The Internal Auditor should assist the Audit team at the time of external auditing Yes No

QAP 7.1.2: Procedure for conducting Management Review Meeting


Q.Management Review Committee should be established comprising of HODs, Quality Coordinator (Principal), Internal Auditor, Head (Accounts section) and other relevant staff of the institute. Yes No
Q.The Principal shall chair the Management Review Meeting Yes No
Q.Management Review Meeting is held quarterly or as and when required. Yes No
Q.Agenda for the review meeting should be prepared and informed to the concerned HOD/Principal and Trainer well in advance Yes No
Q.Review the progress and status of corrective & preventive actions based on Audit Report. Yes No
Q.Summary of internal quality audits & follow up is tabled by the Internal Auditor Yes No
Q.Feedback from industry/ employers are discussed during the Management Review Meeting Yes No
Q.Discuss on the Internal Audit report on the performance of Quality system Yes No
Q.Minutes are recorded and distributed by the Internal Auditor Yes No
